Lighter Account Types
=====================
Source: https://apidocs.lighter.xyz/docs/account-types
Last updated: 2026-03-27

=== STANDARD ACCOUNT (Default) ===

Designed for retail and latency-insensitive traders.
  Maker Fee:            0%
  Taker Fee:            0%
  Taker Latency:        300 ms
  Maker/Cancel Latency: 200 ms

=== PREMIUM ACCOUNT (Opt-in) ===

Designed for high-frequency trading. 0ms maker/cancel latency.
Base fees without LIT staking: 0.0040% maker / 0.0280% taker, 200ms taker latency.

LIT Staking Discount Tiers (fee credits also count as staked LIT):
  Staked LIT  | Discount | Maker Fee | Taker Fee | Taker Latency | sendTx/min
  ------------|----------|-----------|-----------|---------------|----------
  0           | --       | 0.0040%   | 0.0280%   | 200 ms        | 4,000
  1,000       | 2.5%     | 0.0039%   | 0.0273%   | 195 ms        | 5,000
  3,000       | 5%       | 0.0038%   | 0.0266%   | 190 ms        | 6,000
  10,000      | 10%      | 0.0036%   | 0.0252%   | 180 ms        | 7,000
  30,000      | 15%      | 0.0034%   | 0.0238%   | 170 ms        | 8,000
  100,000     | 20%      | 0.0032%   | 0.0224%   | 160 ms        | 12,000
  300,000     | 25%      | 0.0030%   | 0.0210%   | 150 ms        | 24,000
  500,000     | 30%      | 0.0028%   | 0.0196%   | 140 ms        | 40,000

Note: The sendTx/min column applies to both sendTx and sendTxBatch endpoints.
Premium accounts have separate rate-limit buckets from the general weighted
request pool (24,000/min). See rate_limits.txt for full details.

=== SWITCHING BETWEEN TIERS ===

Use the /changeAccountTier endpoint. Requirements:
  - No open positions
  - No open orders
  - At least 24 hours since the last tier change

=== FEE COLLECTION ===

Isolated margin: Fees deducted from the position itself; auto-transfers from
cross margin if needed.
Cross margin: Fees always deducted directly from available cross balance.
Sub-accounts inherit the tier of their main L1 address.
